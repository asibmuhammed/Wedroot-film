Our Story

I’m Muhammed Asib, the founder of Wedroot Films, with over 6 years of experience in the world of wedding photography and filmmaking. My style blends natural storytelling with cinematic elegance, allowing me to capture both the grand moments and the subtle emotions that define each celebration.

I believe weddings are not just events — they are memories, relationships, and stories waiting to be preserved. And behind this vision stands a dedicated team of talented creatives who share the same passion for crafting heartfelt, timeless wedding visuals. Together, we work seamlessly to ensure every couple receives an experience that is warm, professional, and beautifully personal.

Through Wedroot Films, our goal is simple — to deliver authentic, emotional, and elegant visuals that couples can treasure for a lifetime.
